Withdraw from Individual protection 2016 allowance

Once you withdraw, you will no longer have Individual protection 2016. This might mean you have to pay a higher rate of tax on your pension.

Use this service to withdraw from your Individual protection 2016 if:

  • you no longer want to have Individual protection 2016
  • you are no longer eligible to have Individual protection 2016
